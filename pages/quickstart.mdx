## 1) Sign in
Go to **app.gostanna.com** and sign in with Google.

## 2) Select workspace
Stanna infers `workspaceId` from your email domain. You can override in **Settings → Workspace**.

## 3) Connect Google
Open **Integrations → Google Workspace** and grant:
- `openid email profile`
- `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly`

## 4) Backfill and sync
Initial backfill fetches the **last 90 days** of emails by default. You can change the window in **Settings**.

## 5) View dashboard
Open **Dashboard** to see:
- Health score
- 7/30/90-day trends
- Drivers and next-best actions

<Tip>Allowlist **https://app.gostanna.com** and your custom domains in API CORS.</Tip>
