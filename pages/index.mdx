Stanna centralizes client signals from Gmail, Slack, HubSpot, and Intercom to compute a **health score**, show **trends**, and suggest **next-best actions**.

**Core ideas**
- Workspace isolation via `workspaceId` (usually your email domain).
- Lightweight ingestion. Read-only scopes where possible.
- Transparent scoring with attributable drivers.

**Who uses Stanna**
- CS leaders tracking risk and renewal.
- ICs prioritizing inbox and accounts.
- Ops teams monitoring data freshness.

<Note>See **Quickstart** to connect Google and populate data in minutes.</Note>
