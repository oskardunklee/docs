# Getting Started with Lars

This guide walks you through your first interaction with Lars, Stanna's AI assistant.

## Quick Start

### 1. Open Lars

Find the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of any page. Click the sparkle icon to open, or press `âŒ˜L` (Cmd+L).

### 2. Ask Your First Question

Try asking: "Which clients are at risk of churning?"

Lars will analyze your clients and provide:
- List of at-risk clients with health scores
- Risk factors for each client
- Suggested actions to mitigate risk

### 3. Review Suggested Actions

Each suggested action shows:
- What the action will do
- Risk level (Low/Medium/High)
- Execute button to perform the action

Click "Execute" to run any action after reviewing it.

## Understanding Responses

Each response from Lars includes:
- **Analysis**: Insights about your clients or situation
- **Suggested Actions**: Recommendations you can execute
- **Context**: Relevant background information

## Example Questions

### Analyze Client Health
- "What's the health status of my enterprise clients?"
- "Show me clients with declining sentiment scores"
- "Which accounts have the highest engagement this month?"

### Identify Risks
- "Which clients are at risk of churning this quarter?"
- "Show me clients who haven't been contacted recently"
- "What clients need my attention this week?"

### Get Recommendations
- "What should I do about TechCorp's declining health score?"
- "Create tasks for following up with at-risk accounts"
- "What should I prioritize today?"

## Best Practices

### Be Specific
Instead of "Help me with clients," try "Show me clients with health scores below 60 who haven't been contacted in 2 weeks"

### Provide Context
Include timeframes and specifics: "For our enterprise clients this quarter..."

### Ask Follow-ups
Build on previous responses: "Can you create tasks for the at-risk clients you just identified?"

### Review Actions
Always review suggested actions before executing, especially medium and high-risk actions

## Settings

Configure Lars in Settings:
- **Auto-execute low-risk actions**: Enable for faster workflow
- **Response style**: Choose brief, detailed, or technical
- **Risk threshold**: Set sensitivity for risk detection
