## Overview

Client management is the heart of Stanna. Every client in your portfolio gets a detailed profile that tracks interactions, sentiment, revenue, and relationship health automatically.

## Client Profiles

Each client profile contains:

### Basic Information
- **Company Name & Domain**: Primary identifiers
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: Current subscription value
- **Tier**: Enterprise, Mid-Market, SMB, etc.
- **Lifecycle Stage**: Onboarding, Active, Renewal, etc.
- **CSM Owner**: Assigned customer success manager

### Health Metrics
- **Sentiment Score**: AI-powered relationship health (0-100)
- **Last Interaction**: Most recent touchpoint date
- **Interaction Frequency**: Communication patterns
- **Renewal Date**: Next contract milestone

## Managing Client Data

### Editing Client Information
1. Click on any client name to open their profile
2. Click the **Edit** button in the top right
3. Update any field and click **Save**

### Adding Custom Fields
You can add custom fields for your specific business needs:
1. Go to **Settings** â†’ **Client Fields**
2. Click **Add Custom Field**
3. Choose field type (text, number, date, dropdown)
4. Set field name and options
5. Apply to existing clients if desired

### Bulk Operations
Select multiple clients to perform bulk actions:
- **Reassign CSM**: Change ownership across multiple clients
- **Update Tier**: Bulk categorization changes
- **Add Tags**: Apply labels to groups of clients
- **Export Data**: Download client information

## Client Interactions

### Viewing Interaction History
Each client profile shows a complete timeline of interactions:
- **Emails**: Sent and received communications
- **Support Tickets**: Help desk interactions
- **Meetings**: Calendar events and notes
- **Phone Calls**: Logged conversations

### Adding Manual Interactions
1. Open any client profile
2. Click **Add Interaction**
3. Select interaction type:
   - Email
   - Phone call
   - Meeting
   - Note
4. Add details and sentiment
5. Save the interaction

## Stakeholder Management

Track multiple contacts within each client organization:

### Adding Stakeholders
1. In the client profile, scroll to **Stakeholders**
2. Click **Add Stakeholder**
3. Enter contact information:
   - Name and title
   - Email address
   - Role/department
   - Influence level
4. Save the stakeholder

### Stakeholder Insights
- **Sentiment Average**: Relationship health with each contact
- **Interaction History**: Communications with specific stakeholders
- **Influence Mapping**: Decision-maker identification

## Client Segmentation

### Using Tags
Organize clients with custom tags:
- **Industry tags**: SaaS, Manufacturing, Healthcare
- **Size tags**: Enterprise, Mid-Market, SMB
- **Status tags**: Onboarding, Active, Churned
- **Custom tags**: Whatever fits your business

### Filtering & Searching
Use the filter panel to find specific clients:
- **Health Score**: Show only at-risk clients
- **MRR Range**: Filter by revenue size
- **Renewal Date**: Upcoming renewals
- **CSM Owner**: Your assigned clients
- **Tags**: Custom categorization

### Saved Views
Create and save common filter combinations:
1. Set up your filters
2. Click **Save View**
3. Name your view (e.g., "My At-Risk Clients")
4. Access quickly from the Views dropdown
