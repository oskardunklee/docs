## Your Personalized Dashboard

The Stanna dashboard is your command center, displaying the most important information for your role and responsibilities.

## Dashboard Layout

Your dashboard features a modern glassmorphism design with floating background orbs and glass-effect cards for optimal visual clarity.

### Key Performance Cards
The main dashboard displays critical metrics in easy-to-scan cards:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Average Health Score" icon="heart-pulse">
    Your portfolio's overall health with trend indicators and percentage changes
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="MRR at Risk" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    Revenue from clients with health scores ≤40, showing immediate attention needed
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Upcoming Renewals" icon="calendar">
    Count and value of renewals in the next 30 days
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Integration Health" icon="link">
    Status of your Gmail, HubSpot, and Intercom connections with last sync times
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### At-Risk Clients Table
A dedicated section highlights clients needing immediate attention:
- **Health scores ≤40**: Color-coded danger indicators
- **Client details**: Name, MRR, assigned CSM
- **Quick actions**: Direct links to client profiles

### Real-time Updates
The dashboard refreshes automatically and shows:
- **Last updated**: Timestamp showing data freshness
- **Time filters**: Toggle between 7-day and 30-day views
- **Interactive elements**: Click any metric to drill down into details

## Customizing Your Dashboard

You can personalize your dashboard by:
1. **Adding widgets**: Click the "+" button to add new metric displays
2. **Rearranging layout**: Drag and drop widgets to your preferred positions
3. **Filtering timeframes**: Adjust date ranges for trend analysis
4. **Setting alerts**: Configure notifications for critical metrics
