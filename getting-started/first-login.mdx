## Accessing Stanna

1. Go to [app.stanna.io](https://app.stanna.io)
2. Enter your email and password
3. If you're part of multiple workspaces, select the appropriate one

## Your First Dashboard View

When you first log in, you'll see your personalized dashboard tailored to your role:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CS Professional">
    - **My Clients**: Your assigned client portfolio
    - **Tasks Due**: Upcoming actions and follow-ups
    - **Recent Interactions**: Latest client communications
    - **Sentiment Alerts**: Clients requiring attention
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="CS Director">
    - **Team Overview**: Performance across all team members
    - **Portfolio Health**: Overall client health scores
    - **Revenue Pipeline**: MRR trends and renewal forecasts
    - **Team Tasks**: Outstanding actions across the team
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Leadership">
    - **Executive Summary**: High-level KPIs and trends
    - **Revenue Metrics**: MRR, churn, and growth metrics
    - **Strategic Insights**: Patterns and recommendations
    - **Team Performance**: CS team effectiveness metrics
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Understanding Client Cards

Each client is displayed with key information at a glance:

<Info>
**Client Health Score**: Ranges from 0-100, with red (0-40), yellow (41-70), and green (71-100) indicating risk levels.
</Info>

- **Sentiment Score**: Overall relationship health
- **Last Interaction**: Most recent touchpoint
- **MRR**: Monthly recurring revenue
- **Renewal Date**: Next contract milestone
- **CSM Owner**: Assigned customer success manager

## Setting Up Your Preferences

<Steps>
  <Step title="Notification Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Notifications** to configure:
    - Email alerts for at-risk clients
    - Task due reminders
    - Weekly digest preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Dashboard Customization">
    Customize your dashboard widgets by:
    - Clicking the gear icon in the top right
    - Selecting which metrics to display
    - Arranging widgets in your preferred order
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Client View Preferences">
    Configure how you want to see client data:
    - Default sorting (by health score, MRR, etc.)
    - Visible columns in client lists
    - Filtering preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Win: Your First Action

Try this to get familiar with Stanna:

1. **Find a client** using the search bar or browse the client list
2. **Click on their name** to open their detailed view
3. **Review their interaction history** to understand the relationship
4. **Create a task** if you notice any follow-up needed
5. **Add a note** about your observations

<Tip>
**Pro tip**: Use tags to organize clients by industry, size, or any custom categories that matter to your business.
</Tip>

## Common First-Day Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see all my clients?">
    If you're a CS Professional, you only see clients assigned to you. Directors and Leadership see the full portfolio. Check with your admin if assignments need adjusting.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How often does data sync?">
    Most integrations sync every 15-30 minutes. You can manually trigger a sync in Settings > Integrations if needed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I customize my dashboard?">
    Yes! Click the gear icon in the top right of your dashboard to add, remove, or rearrange widgets.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if sentiment scores seem wrong?">
    Sentiment analysis improves over time. You can manually adjust scores and provide feedback to help the AI learn your business context.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
