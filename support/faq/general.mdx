## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Stanna and who is it for?">
    Stanna is a customer success platform designed for CS professionals, directors, and leadership teams. It automatically tracks client health, sentiment, and interactions to help you:
    - Identify at-risk accounts before they churn
    - Automate follow-up tasks and workflows  
    - Track revenue and renewal opportunities
    - Gain insights across your entire client portfolio
    
    **Best for**: B2B SaaS companies, agencies, and service providers with recurring revenue models.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?">
    Most teams are up and running within 30 minutes:
    - **Basic setup**: 10 minutes (account, team, basic settings)
    - **First integration**: 10-15 minutes (HubSpot, Intercom, etc.)
    - **Data sync**: 15-30 minutes (depending on data volume)
    
    We offer free setup calls to help you get started faster.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What integrations do you support?">
    **Current integrations**:
    - HubSpot (contacts, deals, activities)
    - Intercom (conversations, contacts)
    - Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar)
    - Stripe (billing data)
    - CSV import for custom data
    
    **Coming soon**: Salesforce, Slack, Zoom, Microsoft 365
    
    Missing an integration? Email [support@gostanna.com](mailto:support@gostanna.com) with your request.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I import existing client data?">
    Yes, multiple ways:
    - **Integrations**: Automatically sync from connected tools
    - **CSV upload**: Use our template to bulk import clients
    - **Manual entry**: Add clients one by one
    - **API**: For custom integrations (Enterprise plans)
    
    We recommend starting with integrations when possible for ongoing sync.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the different user roles?">
    **CS Professional**:
    - Manages assigned client portfolio
    - Creates and completes tasks
    - Views client interactions and health scores
    - Limited to assigned accounts
    
    **CS Director**:
    - Full team and portfolio visibility
    - Team performance analytics
    - Can reassign clients and manage workflows
    - Advanced reporting features
    
    **Leadership (CEO/COO)**:
    - Executive dashboard with high-level KPIs
    - Strategic insights and trend analysis
    - Revenue and growth metrics
    - Cross-team visibility
    
    **Admin**:
    - All Leadership permissions plus
    - Workspace settings and integrations
    - User management and billing
    - Data export and security settings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan later?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime:
    - **Upgrades**: Take effect immediately with prorated billing
    - **Downgrades**: Take effect at next billing cycle
    - **User changes**: Add/remove users monthly
    
    Contact [support@gostanna.com](mailto:support@gostanna.com) for custom arrangements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for annual plans?">
    Yes! Annual plans include:
    - **2 months free** (16.7% discount)
    - **Priority support** and success manager
    - **Advanced features** like custom reports
    - **Free setup and training** sessions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    - **Data retention**: 90 days after cancellation
    - **Export options**: Download all data before canceling
    - **Reactivation**: Easy to restart within 90 days
    - **No cancellation fees** or long-term contracts
    
    We also offer downgrades and pauses for temporary needs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Functionality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does health scoring work?">
    Our AI analyzes multiple factors to create a 0-100 health score:
    
    **Communication patterns** (40%):
    - Response times and frequency
    - Email sentiment analysis
    - Meeting attendance and engagement
    
    **Support activity** (25%):
    - Ticket volume and severity
    - Resolution times
    - Escalation patterns
    
    **Business indicators** (25%):
    - Payment history and timing
    - Contract renewal proximity
    - Usage patterns (if integrated)
    
    **Relationship depth** (10%):
    - Stakeholder engagement
    - Strategic vs tactical conversations
    - Executive involvement
    
    Scores update daily and improve accuracy over time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I customize health scoring?">
    **Current customization**:
    - Manual sentiment corrections
    - Custom client tags and segments
    - Industry-specific weightings
    
    **Enterprise features**:
    - Custom scoring algorithms
    - Industry-specific models
    - Integration with your product analytics
    
    Contact us about custom scoring for your business model.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How accurate is sentiment analysis?">
    Our sentiment analysis achieves **~85% accuracy** out of the box and improves to **90%+** with your corrections:
    
    **What it analyzes**:
    - Email content and tone
    - Support ticket language
    - Meeting notes and summaries
    
    **Improvement tips**:
    - Correct obviously wrong sentiment scores
    - Add context notes to interactions
    - Include industry-specific terminology in settings
    
    The AI learns from your corrections to better understand your business context.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What kind of tasks can be automated?">
    Stanna can automatically create tasks based on:
    
    **Risk triggers**:
    - Health score drops below threshold
    - No interaction in X days
    - Negative sentiment spike
    
    **Renewal workflows**:
    - Schedule QBR 90 days before renewal
    - Create renewal prep tasks 60 days out
    - Set contract negotiation reminders
    
    **Engagement patterns**:
    - Follow up after no email response
    - Schedule check-in after support tickets
    - Create expansion tasks for healthy accounts
    
    **Custom rules**: Enterprise plans can create custom automation rules.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Data & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How secure is my data?">
    Security is our top priority:
    
    **Infrastructure**:
    - SOC 2 Type II compliant
    - Data encrypted at rest and in transit
    - Hosted on AWS with 99.9% uptime SLA
    
    **Access controls**:
    - Role-based permissions
    - SSO integration available
    - Audit logs for all actions
    
    **Data protection**:
    - GDPR and CCPA compliant
    - Regular security audits
    - Employee background checks
    
    See our [Security page](https://stanna.io/security) for detailed information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Who can see my client data?">
    **Within your workspace**:
    - **CS Professionals**: Only assigned clients
    - **CS Directors**: Full portfolio view
    - **Leadership**: Portfolio analytics, not detailed interactions
    - **Admins**: Full access to manage workspace
    
    **Stanna employees**:
    - **No access** to your data without explicit permission
    - **Support access** only when you grant it for troubleshooting
    - **Anonymized analytics** to improve the product (opt-out available)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes, multiple export options:
    
    **Self-service exports**:
    - Client lists and profiles
    - Interaction histories
    - Task and activity reports
    - Health score trends
    
    **Full data exports**:
    - Complete database export (Admin only)
    - Available in CSV, JSON formats
    - Includes all historical data
    
    **API access**: Enterprise plans include full API for custom exports.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long do you keep my data?">
    **Active accounts**: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
    
    **Canceled accounts**: 
    - 90 days retention for easy reactivation
    - Export available during this period
    - Complete deletion after 90 days
    
    **GDPR requests**: Immediate data deletion upon request
    
    **Backups**: Encrypted backups kept for disaster recovery (30 days max)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Usage & Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many clients can I manage?">
    No hard limits, but recommendations by role:
    
    **CS Professional**: 15-30 clients for optimal attention
    **CS Director**: 50-100 client portfolio oversight
    **Enterprise teams**: Unlimited with proper team structure
    
    Our pricing scales with team size, not client count.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How often should I check Stanna?">
    **Daily routine** (15 minutes):
    - Review at-risk client alerts
    - Complete due tasks
    - Scan overnight interactions
    
    **Weekly review** (30 minutes):
    - Portfolio health analysis
    - Upcoming renewal preparation
    - Team performance review (Directors)
    
    **Monthly deep-dive** (1 hour):
    - Strategic account planning
    - Health score trend analysis
    - Workflow optimization
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I have multiple products/teams?">
    **Multiple workspaces**: Separate client portfolios by product/team
    **Cross-workspace analytics**: Enterprise feature for consolidated reporting
    **Shared clients**: Tag and segment clients across multiple offerings
    **Role flexibility**: Users can have different roles in different workspaces
    
    Contact us to discuss multi-product setups.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
